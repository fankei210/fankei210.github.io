  • Harlequin Ebook Subscription

    UX/UI, Design

    The goal of Harlequin E-Book Subscription is to provide information and help readers to subscribe E-books more efficiently.

    The project was developed with Agile Development Process in sprints. At the beginning of a sprint, we conducted in depth user research with personas to better our understanding of user journey. During our exploration, we created a number of wireframes and performed multiple usability tests with our users to refine our site flow. At the end of a sprint, we gathered and used all the feedback from our users to design, build and release the product for public testing. After making many adjustments from failures in every sprite, we finally launched the new Harlequin E-book Subscription that was positively received by our users.
