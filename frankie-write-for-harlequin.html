  • Write for Harlequin

    UX/UI, Strategy, Design, Branding

    Write for Harlequin is the voice of Harlequin's romance editors. It is the place where editors share their editorial and publishing expertise and it's an invaluable resource for writers who are interested in being published by Harlequin. Designed to encourage romance authors on their journey to being published, writers will find information on each Harlequin romance series, guides and FAQs on how to submit a story for consideration, and updates on Harlequin's author outreach initiatives.

Project Goals

  • Help writers to find information on each Harlequin romance series

  • Guiding writers how to submit a story for consideration

  • Provide updates on Harlequin's author outreach initiatives

  • Enrich the experience of interaction between editors and writers

Research Methods

  • Persona

  • User Journey Mapping

  • Site Mapping

  • Information Architecture

  • User Interviews

  • Usability Testing

  • Information architecture exercise plays an important role in this project. A balance between Breadth vs. Depth is needed for users to find information they need efficiently.
